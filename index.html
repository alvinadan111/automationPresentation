PO-Mailer v2 Automation

📤 PO-Mailer v2

📤 Sends Purchase Orders (POs) to Vendors Automatically

🧾 Gets PO info + PDF from SmartSheet
📧 Sends email to vendor with PO attachment
❌ No more manual email sending!
SmartSheet name:
  • 📊 BES Purchasing Log

🧩 Executive Summary

Flow Visual

1
SmartSheet Check

Find rows with: PO Status = NEW, Has attachment, PO Issued

2
Download PO PDF

Retrieve purchase order document

3
Send Email to Vendor

Automated email delivery with PO details

4
Update Status to "SENT"

Mark as processed in SmartSheet

5
Log Activity to Slack

Channel: #po-mailer-v2

👉 Runs every 30 minutes

📥 Input Conditions

Triggers When Row Has:

✅ PO Mailer Status = NEW
📎 Has PDF Attachment
🧾 PO Status = PO Issued
🕒 Runs every 30 minutes automatically

📤 Expected Output

📧 Vendor receives email with:

📝 Purchase Details
📎 Attached PDF PO

⚠️ Error Handling

If any errors occur during the process, the automation:

💬 Sends a message to the #po-mailer-v2 Slack channel with details
📊 Sets the PO Mailer Status to "ERROR" in the SmartSheet log
